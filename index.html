Software Engineer | Lockheed Martin

As an engineer on LM Space's ISIM team for SBIRS/OPIR, I was deeply involved in developing a range of critical features and support tools. Among these accomplishments, I spearheaded the creation of essential ISIM functionalities like the Active Ports Display and the COMs State Display Color Coding. These endeavors have not only enriched our team's capabilities but have also significantly enhanced the effectiveness and reliability of our systems.

As an intern on the LM Enterprise Operations OTD team, I focused on my intern project and various smaller engineering tasks.
